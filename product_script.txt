1. CORE PLATFORM MECHANICS (THE MODEL)
--------------------------------------
ConnectCrypto is a high-performance, administrative-led P2P trading ecosystem
designed for seamless USDT/INR liquidation.

- ROLE BASED ACCESS:
  * CLIENTS: Sell crypto (USDT/ETH/BNB) at fixed institutional rates.
  * TRADERS: Verified liquidity nodes providing the buy orders.
  * ADMIN (ROOT): Monitors every transaction, approves withdrawals, and manages 
    the Global Gateway Protocol.

- GLOBAL GATEWAY PROTOCOL (MASTER CONTROL):
  The platform features a unique "Reroute" capability. When active, all 
  payments across the entire network are redirected from individual trader 
  wallets to the Admin's Master Wallets. This ensures 100% security and 
  institutional control over liquidity.

- INSTITUTIONAL BONUS:
  The model incentivizes high-volume trades. Any transaction over 500 USDT 
  automatically triggers a 2% "Global Protocol Bonus," which is added to the 
  client's final settlement amount.

- SETTLEMENT FLOW:
  1. Client picks a market position (Offer).
  2. Client transfers crypto to the provided QR/Wallet.
  3. Client uploads Transaction Hash (TXID).
  4. Admin/Trader verifies on-chain and marks as "Success".
  5. Fiat balance is instantly credited to the Client's protocol wallet.
  6. Client requests withdrawal to UPI or Bank Account (Processed in 30 mins).
