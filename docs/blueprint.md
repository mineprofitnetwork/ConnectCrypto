# **App Name**: ConnectCrypto

## Core Features:

- Secure User Authentication: Robust registration, login, and password recovery functionalities for both Clients (sellers) and Traders (buyers) to ensure secure access to the platform.
- Client P2P Sell Offers: Clients can easily create, view, and manage their offers to sell cryptocurrencies, specifying the amount, preferred exchange rate, and their chosen fiat payment methods (e.g., UPI, Bank Transfer).
- Trader P2P Offer Matching & Fiat Payment: Traders can browse available sell offers from Clients, accept them, initiate fiat payments to the Client's chosen method, and upload proof of payment for verification.
- Trader Crypto Wallet Integration (MetaMask): Traders can securely connect their MetaMask extension to import and manage multiple crypto receiving wallet addresses and corresponding QR codes for various coins and network to facilitate receiving crypto payments.
- Fiat Payment Method Management: Both Clients and Traders can securely add and manage their Bank account details and UPI IDs, making it easy to receive or send fiat payments during trades.
- Admin Control Panel: A dedicated admin portal allowing administrators to oversee and manage all registered P2P traders, user accounts, platform information, and monitor ongoing trade activities for dispute resolution.
- AI-powered Trade Assistant: An AI tool that assists traders by generating clear, context-specific instructions for sellers on how to send crypto, or confirmation messages for buyers once crypto is received, based on trade details and chosen payment methods.

## Style Guidelines:

- The overall color scheme is dark, evoking a sense of professionalism and modern technology. The primary color, #664CE6, is a vibrant violet-blue, symbolizing trust and innovation. It provides a striking contrast against the dark background.
- The background color is a deep, muted indigo (#222026), offering a sophisticated base that enhances readability and keeps focus on content.
- The accent color, #85A3FB, is a lighter, refreshing blue, used for interactive elements like buttons, links, and highlights, ensuring clear calls to action and visual distinction.
- Headlines will utilize 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, tech-savvy, and slightly geometric appearance. Body text will use 'Inter' (sans-serif), ensuring excellent readability for transactional data and general information.
- Utilize modern, sleek, and minimalist iconography with a slight edge or 'glowing' effect where appropriate, aligning with themes of cryptocurrency and secure digital transactions. Icons should clearly represent actions like 'connect wallet', 'deposit', 'withdraw', and 'trade'.
- The layout will feature clean, responsive grid systems adaptable for both mobile and desktop screens. Generous spacing, clear card-based components for listings and offers, and well-defined sections will improve information hierarchy and user experience.
- Implement subtle and functional animations, such as smooth transitions for content loading, interactive element states (hovers, clicks), and form submissions, to provide positive user feedback without distracting from the core functionality.
